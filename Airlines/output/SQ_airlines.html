Singapore Airlines: What Every Traveller Should Know

Four Cabin Classes

Suites, Business, Premium Economy, and Economy. Suites on select A380 routes rank among the finest private air travel experiences in the world.

Baggage Allowance

Economy typically gets 25–30 kg checked plus 7 kg carry-on; Business and Suites enjoy higher allowances. Always verify on your booking confirmation.

Check In Online — Free

Online check-in opens 48 hours before departure and is free. SQ charges an airport check-in fee at select airports, so check in via the app or website.

Earn miles on SQ and Star Alliance partners, redeem for flights and upgrades. Elite tiers run from Elite Silver to the invitation-calibre PPS Club.
